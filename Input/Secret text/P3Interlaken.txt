Interlaken - Beautiful Village between two lakes

Interlaken is located in the Bernese Oberland between
Lake Thun and Lake Brienz ,  on an alluvial plain called
Boedeli . The two lakes are connected by the Aare ,
which flows through Interlaken .

Interlaken is a Swiss town and municipality in the
Interlaken-Oberhasli administrative district
in the canton of Bern . It is an important and well-
known tourist destination in the Bernese Oberland
region of the Swiss Alps , and the main transport
gateway to the mountains and lakes of that region .

The official language of Interlaken is German ,but
the main spoken language is the local variant of the
Alemannic Swiss German dialect , Bernese German .
